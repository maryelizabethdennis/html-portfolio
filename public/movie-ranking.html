The Best Movies (According to Ellie)

My top 3 movies of all-time.



The Princess Bride

This is my favourite movie of all time. It is definitely a cult-classic now but when I was a child I would watch it with my sister at my grandparents' house

Pride and Prejudice (2005)

I used to watch this all the time with my mother when I was in high school. We would giggle and say "Mr. Bingley" to each other with a huge emphasis on the "B".

Spider-Man Into the Spider-Verse

I absolutely love the animation styles used in this, it really showed how hard the creators worked on this as a project.
